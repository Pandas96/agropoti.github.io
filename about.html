About Us

All details about our organization Agropoti Producer Company Limited and our Vision and our mission.

Background & Objective

About Agropoti Producer Company Limited.

Formed in the year 2025 and incorporated under the companies act of 2013 (18 of 2013). APCL is owned and run by the farmers. It was set up to embolden the farmers to take on the challenges of production, procurement, processing, and marketing of agricultural products and goods.

The company aims to cut out the middleman and hence ensures that the farmers benefit the maximum in the end pricing of their agricultural products. APCL is registered with the West Bengal Agricultural Marketing Department as well as the e-Nam (electronic-National agriculture market) platform.

Our Vision

We are dedicated to enhancing the profit of farmers, promoting equitable growth, and ensuring financial security in the farming community of the region. Through collaborative efforts, efficient resource management, and market-driven strategies.

We aim to provide farmers in West Bengal with access to modern techniques, technology, and markets. By fostering innovation, knowledge sharing, and collective decision-making.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a sustainable agricultural ecosystem that empowers farmers and uplifts rural communities. We aim to enhance agricultural productivity, promote income growth, and ensure food security for the state.

We strive to create a vibrant and prosperous agricultural sector that serves as a model for sustainable rural development in West Bengal.
