Our Story

West Bengal's sustainable Farmers' producer company since 2025

Agropoti Producer Company Limited (APCL), office in Jamalpur, Purba Bardhaman, West Bengal is the first FPO in the block owned by the farmers. We serve more than 100 primary producer members in West Bengal. APCL was founded in the year 2025 and incorporated under the Companies Act 2013. We are dedicated to bringing technological innovation and high-yield organic methods directly to the grassroot level.

Our Farm

By integrating modern, eco-friendly farming practices with traditional care, we ensure our Purba Bardhaman fields thrive sustainably. Our cooperative model enables smallholder farmers to access high-quality equipment, seed culture training, and direct trade markets without middlemen.
