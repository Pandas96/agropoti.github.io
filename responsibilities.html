Responsibilities

We believe that care for the environment and communities is fundamental to our business and integral to its success.

Sustainability Commitments

On behalf of Agropoti Producer Company Limited, we believe that care for the environment and communities is fundamental to our business and integral to its success. We, therefore, invest substantially and continually to ensure that the environment we operate in is enriched by our activities and that any social or environmental damage caused by our actions is minimized.

Promoting Agricultural Activities

Encourage and engage in agricultural production, procurement, processing, marketing, and other related activities to enhance agricultural output across Jamalpur block.

Facilitating Crop Planning

Assist members in planning crop cycles, selecting appropriate resilient crops, and adopting efficient farming practices for optimal yields and high ecological balances.

Technology & Innovation

Promote the use of modern agricultural technologies, advanced bio-machinery, and best practices to increase productivity, efficiency, and sustainability in farming operations.

Capacity Building & Training

Organize training programs, workshops, and awareness campaigns to educate members about advancements in agriculture, market trends, and financial literacy.

Market Linkages & Negotiation

Facilitate direct market linkages and negotiate fair prices for agricultural produce, ensuring that members get highly competitive rates for their crops without brokers.

Quality Control & Standards

Encourage strict adherence to quality standards, organic certifications, and grading systems to enhance the marketability of agricultural products and ensure consumer satisfaction.

"By fulfilling these responsibilities, an Agricultural Producer Company plays a crucial role in advancing the agricultural sector, supporting farmers, and contributing to the overall socio-economic development of rural areas."
